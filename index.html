About Liz

Liz Kern Designs is a full-service design company that creates environments for corporate and private clients requiring unique solutions. From custom booth design and special events to interiors and sourcing, her company delivers on exacting specifications with an equal balance of creativity and professionalism. As CEO and founder, Liz Kern brings nearly two decades of experience to every job, managing a team of highly trained professionals in order to execute tolutions quickly and effectively. Liz and her team have worked with hundreds of designers and trade show organizers to create showrooms and display merchandising with a proven record of driving sales up. As a home interior design professional, Liz brings the same passion and creative drive to her private clients, creating well organized, beautiful spaces that bring out the best in any home and represents the client's personality.
